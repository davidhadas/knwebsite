Knative Components

Knative has two main components that can work together to empower teams working with Kubernetes. Serving and
Eventing work together to automate and manage to deploy your containers.

{{ end }}

Run containers in Kubernetes with ease by manging a single resource per container. Let Knative take care of the details of networking, autoscaling, revision control and security. Teams can focus on core logic using any programming language.

You may optionaly use Knative's embedded real-time streaming technology to handle communication to your kubernetes loosely coupled services. Let Knative auto create and maintain a topic for queuing and distributing your incoming service events.
